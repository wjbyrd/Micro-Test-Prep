How This Works

Choose a dungeon to begin reviewing.

Each dungeon focuses on a different part of the course and adapts to your performance as you play.

  • Correct answers move you forward.
  • Missed questions may send you back or trigger review.
  • Boss rooms check whether you are ready to advance.
  • Completion is reported when you finish a dungeon
  • Progress is saved at your current room. You can close the browser and pick up where you last were.

When you enter a dungeon, use your school email on that game’s start screen so your progress can be recorded.
