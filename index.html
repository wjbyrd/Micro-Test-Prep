How This Works

Choose a gauntlet to begin reviewing.

Each gauntlet focuses on a different part of the course and adapts to your performance as you play.

  • Correct answers move you forward.
  • Missed questions may send you back or trigger review.
  • Boss rooms check whether you are ready to advance.
  • Completion is reported when you finish a gauntlet.
